Polecat Starts Motorcycle in Luib

{% now "jS F Y H:i" %}

Polecats are rarely seen in Scotland, and even rarer in the West Highlands. That is until recently, when one of our readers spotted a wild
polecat in their shed attempting to start their Suzuki motorcycle. It is still unclear if the polecat in question is wild or an escaped pet. The polecat
has been remanded in custody and is awaiting trial for attempted theft.

Happy Birthday Luther Vandross 20th April 2021

Today marks what would be the American singer, Luther Vandross' 70th birthday. Mr Vandross is remembered fondly in town halls across the highlands, where his
hits were played many a time at the end of Friday Ceidlihs across the west Highlands of Scotland.


Fallen Tree on A85 Near Glen Lochy

{% now "jS F Y H:i" %}

A large Scots pine tree has fallen on to the A85 near Glen Lochy. This has been reported to Stirlingshire Council who are currently attempting to move
the fallen tree from the route. We kindly advise our readers to try and take a diversion if intending to travel through Glen Lochy to avoid delays.
